Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

This extremely simple, yet delicious sandwich is an extremely fast one to make. In modern year, it seems people need instructions to make even the simplest things.. even PB&J.

Ingredients:

  • 2x Slice of Bread
  • 1 1/2Tbsp of Peanut Butter
  • 1/2 Tbsp of Grape Jelly
  • 1/2 Tbsp of Grape Jam

Directions:

  1. Pull the bread out of the bag
  2. Place the bread onto the counter top
  3. Pull out two butter knives. This will be used for spreading the ingredients.
  4. Take your peanut butter, and spread it onto one of the slices of bread
  5. Take your Jelly and Jam, and mix the two inside of a small bowl
  6. Gently pour the mixture onto the slice of bread that does not have anything on it yet
  7. Put the two slices of bread together, with the ingredients that you have spread facing each other
  8. Enjoy!
